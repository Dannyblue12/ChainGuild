Introduction

Web3 and GameFi are rapidly evolving, but they remain largely disconnected from the traditional gaming world. ChainGuild is here to change that. We aim to become the leading gateway for traditional gamers to step into the world of Web3 and GameFi, while simultaneously empowering developers with the tools, audience, and feedback they need to create next-generation games.

Why Now?

GameFi is on the rise, but most gamers still have no idea where to begin. ChainGuild emerges at a critical moment—when Web3 technologies need mass adoption, and gamers are actively searching for new ways to be rewarded for the time they spend playing.

Mission

Our mission is to onboard more people into Web3 through the engaging world of GameFi. We strive to increase awareness and understanding of GameFi, making it accessible and exciting for everyday gamers. At our core, we're a community-first project.
